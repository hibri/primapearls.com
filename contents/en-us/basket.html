Payment methods

We accept payments using PayPal. You don't need to have a Paypal Account you can simply enter your credit or debit card details on a Secure Payments Site and they will carry out the transaction for you. There is no charge for this service.

To make sure your payment is handled securely once you click on Make a Payment a fresh Paypal Payment window will open. If you have a PopUp Blocker on your computer you will need to temporarily allow Pop Ups for the PayPal payment site to appear.
